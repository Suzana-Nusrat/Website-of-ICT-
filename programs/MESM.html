Objective

To produce professionals with practical skills and competencies blended with theoretical and methodological capabilities to contribute to the environmental sector.

Mission

To produce professionals and enhance career opportunity for the people of different backgrounds with overlapping social, ecological and economic issues.

Vision

• To provide specialized and focused knowledge, skills and tools relevant to the field of environment. • To build appropriate attitude and leadership for an inclusive, environmental friendly sound society. • To develop skilled professionals to lead the nation toward environmental sustainability.
